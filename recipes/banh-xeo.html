Banh Xeo Recipe

Ingredients

  • 200g rice flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 200g pork belly, thinly sliced
  • 200g shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups bean sprouts
  • 1 bunch fresh herbs (mint, basil, cilantro)
  • Vegetable oil

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix the rice flour, turmeric powder, coconut milk, water, and salt to make the batter. Let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat a bit of oil in a non-stick pan over medium heat. Add a few slices of pork belly, shrimp, and onion. Cook until they start to turn golden.
  3. Pour a ladle of batter into the pan, swirling it around to cover the bottom. Cover and cook for about 2 minutes, until the edges start to crisp up.
  4. Add a handful of bean sprouts to one side of the pan, cover again and cook for another 2 minutes.
  5. Fold the pancake in half, then slide it onto a plate.
  6. Repeat with the remaining ingredients.
  7. Serve the pancakes with fresh herbs and dipping sauce.
