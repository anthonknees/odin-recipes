Bo Luc Lac Recipe

Ingredients

  • 500g beef sirloin
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 bunch watercress
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Cut the beef into 1-inch cubes. Marinate with garlic, oyster sauce, soy sauce, sugar, and cornstarch for at least 15 minutes.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a pan over high heat.
  3. Add the beef and sear on all sides until browned but still pink in the middle.
  4. Remove the beef from the pan and set aside.
  5. In the same pan, add the onion and tomatoes and cook until softened.
  6. Return the beef to the pan and stir to combine.
  7. Serve the beef over a bed of watercress.
